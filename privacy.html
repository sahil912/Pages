Word Safari - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Quick Summary: Word Safari does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your child's data stays completely private on their device.

1. Information Collection

Word Safari is designed with privacy as a top priority. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information, including:

  • No names or email addresses
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or similar technologies
  • No photographs or media

2. Local Data Storage

Word Safari stores game progress locally on your device only, including:

  • Words spelled
  • Animals discovered
  • Game progress and achievements
  • User preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. Internet Connection

Word Safari works 100% offline. The app does not require or use an internet connection for any functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

Word Safari does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics services
  • No social media platforms
  • No cloud storage services

5. Children's Privacy

Word Safari is designed for children ages 6-12 and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or anyone else.

6. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access. All game data remains securely stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's security features.

7. In-App Purchases and Advertising

Word Safari contains:

  • No in-app purchases
  • No advertisements
  • No external links (except for support email)

8. Notifications

Word Safari may request permission to send local notifications for:

  • Daily learning reminders
  • Achievement celebrations
  • Streak maintenance alerts

These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any data transmission. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete all Word Safari data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset Progress" option in the app settings, or
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

This will permanently remove all locally stored game progress.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Word Safari after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Your Rights

Since Word Safari does not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete on our end. All game data is stored exclusively on your device and is under your complete control.

Summary: Word Safari is a completely private, offline educational app. We respect your family's privacy and have designed the app to keep all data on your device. No information is ever collected, transmitted, or shared.
