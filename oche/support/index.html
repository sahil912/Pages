Support

We're happy to help. Most questions are answered below.

How do I enter scores?

Tap a player's visit using the keypad. Use Quick total to enter your three-dart total in one go, or Per-dart to tap each dart (single / double / treble, plus 25 and the 50). Per-dart entry unlocks precise checkout and doubles stats.

What does the green panel mean?

That's the checkout. The instant a finish is reachable, Oche shows the standard out — for example T20 T20 50. It glows green so you never miss it.

I tapped the wrong score — can I fix it?

Yes. Undo steps back one dart at a time and is always reliable, even across a completed visit.

What's free, and what's in Oche Pro?

x01 scoring (501/301/701) with live checkouts and no ads is free forever. Oche Pro is a one-time $6.99 unlock that adds Cricket, training games, advanced stats, in-person tournaments and the AirPlay big-screen scoreboard. No subscription.

I bought Pro on another device / reinstalled.

Open Settings → Restore purchase. Your one-time unlock is tied to your Apple ID and restores for free.

Where is my data stored?

Everything — match history, player stats, tournaments and practice bests — is stored privately on your iPhone. There's no account and no sign-up.

How do I put the match on a TV?

With Oche Pro, use AirPlay to mirror to an Apple TV or AirPlay display and the scoreboard appears full-screen for the room.
