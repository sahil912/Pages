Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

FieldReport is designed to be private by default. Your inspection data stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. FieldReport does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There is no account, no sign-up, and no analytics or tracking.

Where your data lives

  • Inspections, findings, photos, templates, and your comment library are stored locally on your device.
  • Reports you generate (PDFs) are created on-device and shared only when you choose to share or export them.
  • FieldReport works fully offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

Photos

Photos you capture or attach are used solely to build your inspection reports and are stored on your device. FieldReport does not upload your photos anywhere.

Purchases

The one-time unlock is processed by Apple through the App Store. FieldReport never sees your payment details. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Third parties

FieldReport contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.
