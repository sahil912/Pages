Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

The short version: PicklePoint runs on your device and syncs only through your own private iCloud. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or store any of your personal data on servers we operate.

What we collect

Nothing. PicklePoint has no account, no sign-up, no analytics, and no advertising or tracking SDKs. We do not collect your name, email, location, or the contents of your matches.

Where your data lives

Your matches, players, ratings, tournaments and open-play sessions are stored on your iPhone using Apple's on-device database. If you are signed in to iCloud, this data syncs across your own Apple devices through Apple's private CloudKit — encrypted and tied to your Apple ID. We have no access to it, and it never passes through any server we control. You can turn iCloud sync off at any time in iOS Settings.

Purchases

The optional one-time Pro unlock is processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We never see your payment details. Restoring a purchase uses Apple's receipt — we store no purchase data ourselves.

Children

PicklePoint is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. It contains no user-generated content shared with others, no ads, and no data collection.
