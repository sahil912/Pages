Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

CricVerse is built to be private by design. Your data stays on your device (and, if you choose, in your own private iCloud). We do not operate servers that store your matches, and we do not have accounts.

What we collect

Nothing. CricVerse does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There is no analytics SDK, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Your data & storage

Your matches, tournaments, teams, squads and stats are stored locally on your device. If you are signed in to iCloud, this data also syncs through your own private iCloud database so it is available on your other Apple devices and survives reinstalling the app or moving to a new device. This sync is handled entirely by Apple’s iCloud; we never see your data and have no servers of our own. You can manage iCloud in iOS Settings, and you can delete your data at any time from within the app.

Purchases

CricVerse offers a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase processed by Apple. We never see your payment information. There is no subscription.

Children

CricVerse is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page.
