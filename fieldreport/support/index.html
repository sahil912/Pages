Frequently asked

How many reports are free?

You can build and export your first 3 complete reports free, with full PDF export. After that, a single one-time purchase unlocks unlimited reports — no subscription.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Capturing findings and photos, inserting comments, annotating, and generating PDFs all work with no internet connection.

Where is my data stored?

On your device. FieldReport has no account and collects no data. Your templates and comment library are yours — exportable any time.

Can I customize the template and comments?

Yes. The Full Home template and the seeded comment library are a starting point you fully control — edit sections, items, and comments to match your workflow and jurisdiction.

Is FieldReport a compliance guarantee?

No. FieldReport provides report structure and example comment text as a starting point. You are solely responsible for the accuracy, completeness, and SOP/jurisdiction compliance of every report you produce.
