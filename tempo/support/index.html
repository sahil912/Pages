Support

We're here to help. Most questions are answered below.

How do I add a task quickly?

Tap the + button and just type. Tempo reads natural language: “Submit report #q3 fri 5pm !!” sets the project (Q3), the date (Friday), the time (5pm), and a priority flag automatically. You can also tap the suggestion chips to add a date, time, or flag.

How does the focus timer work?

Open Focus, pick a length (25/5, 50/10, or 15/3), and tap Start — or start a session from any task's detail screen. Completed sessions build your daily streak and feed your weekly stats.

How do repeating tasks work?

Open a task, tap the schedule chip, and choose a Repeat (daily, weekdays, weekly, or monthly). When you complete a repeating task it's checked off for that day and a fresh copy appears on the next date — so you keep the sense of progress and never lose the rhythm.

Do overdue tasks disappear?

No. Anything you didn't finish shows in an Overdue section at the top of Today so it stays in view until you handle it.

Does my data sync across devices?

Yes, privately. If you're signed in to iCloud, your tasks sync across your own Apple devices through Apple's encrypted CloudKit. There's no account to create and nothing passes through our servers. See our Privacy Policy.

Is Tempo really free?

Yes — completely. No subscription, no paywall, no ads, and no in-app purchases. Every feature is included.

How do I add the widget?

Long-press your Home Screen, tap +, search for Tempo, and pick a size. There are Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets showing your progress, streak, and next tasks.

Can I switch to dark mode?

Yes. Go to Settings → Appearance and choose Light, Dark, or Auto, plus an accent color.
