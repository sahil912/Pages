Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Logbook collects no personal data. There is no account, no tracking, no ads, and no analytics. Your logbook stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Logbook has no servers and no account system. The flights, aircraft, certificates and settings you enter are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us or to any third party.

Tracking & analytics

Logbook does not track you, does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not collect any usage data or device identifiers.

Notifications

If you enable currency reminders, Logbook schedules local notifications on your device. These are generated and delivered entirely on-device; no notification content leaves your phone.

Purchases

Logbook Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple. We never see your payment details. Purchase validation is handled by Apple's App Store; please see Apple's privacy policy for how Apple handles transactions.

Importing & exporting

When you import a logbook file or export a PDF/CSV, that processing happens entirely on your device. We do not receive or store any of your logbook data.

Children's privacy

Logbook is rated 4+ and does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date above will change. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
