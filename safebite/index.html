A second pair of eyes

SafeBite reads the label with your camera and checks it against your allergens — entirely on your device, fully offline.

Download on the App Store

SafeBite is a second pair of eyes for reading ingredient labels — not a replacement for reading them. It can miss allergens and can't detect hidden cross-contact, so always confirm on the label. Not a medical device.

Built for the highest-stakes read

📷

On-device OCR

Reads the actual printed label with Apple's Vision framework. No barcode database to be stale or wrong.

🔒

Private & offline

No photo or label text ever leaves your phone. No account needed. Works in any grocery aisle, no signal required.

🧬

Hidden-name matching

A curated dictionary maps derivative names — casein, whey, albumin, semolina, tahini — back to the allergen they belong to.

👨‍👩‍👧

Profiles & custom allergens

Unlimited custom allergens and a profile per family member, with one-tap switching from the scan screen.

⚠️

Honest by design

Never a green "safe" all-clear. Clear results — found, no match (read the label), or couldn't read — that always send you back to the label.
