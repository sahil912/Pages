Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

The short version: VolleyScore runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or store any of your data. Your matches and tournaments never leave your iPhone.

Who we are

VolleyScore ("the app", "we", "us") is a volleyball scoring and tournament app for iOS. It lets you score matches and run tournaments with groups, standings, and knockout brackets — entirely without an account or any connection to our servers. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data we collect

None. VolleyScore does not collect personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not operate servers of our own. Your teams, matches, and tournament data are never uploaded to us.
  • We do not require an account, email, or any personal information.
  • We do not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

Where your data lives

All app data — team names, scores, tournaments, brackets, and your settings — is stored in the app's private storage on your iPhone. It is not transmitted to us or to any third party, and it works fully offline.

Purchases

VolleyScore offers a one-time, non-subscription in-app purchase ("VolleyScore Pro") to unlock unlimited tournaments. Purchases and restores are handled entirely by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We never receive or store your payment details. Apple's handling of transactions is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

VolleyScore does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above.
