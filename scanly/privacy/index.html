Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

The short version: Scanly runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or store any of your data. Your documents and their contents never leave your iPhone.

Who we are

Scanly ("the app", "we", "us") is a document-scanner utility for iOS that turns paper documents, receipts, and photos into searchable PDFs using on-device text recognition. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data we collect

None. Scanly does not collect personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not have servers, and your scans, documents, and recognized text are never uploaded anywhere.
  • We do not require an account, sign-in, or any personal information.
  • We do not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

Camera, Photos & Files access

To do its job, Scanly asks your permission to use the camera (to scan pages) and to import images or PDFs you choose from Photos or Files. This access is used solely to capture and process your documents on your device. Optical character recognition (OCR) is performed locally using Apple's on-device Vision framework. No image, page, PDF, or recognized text is ever sent off your device.

Your scanned documents are stored only in Scanly's private storage on your device. You can delete any document at any time from within the app.

Purchases

Scanly offers a one-time, non-subscription in-app purchase ("Scanly Pro") to unlock unlimited documents. Purchases and restores are handled entirely by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We never receive or store your payment details. Apple's handling of transactions is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

Scanly does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data retention & security

Because we never collect or transmit your data, there is nothing for us to retain or expose. All app data and documents remain on your device under iOS's standard protections.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.
