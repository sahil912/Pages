Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

The short version: ClearSpace runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or store any of your data. Your photos never leave your iPhone.

Who we are

ClearSpace ("the app", "we", "us") is a photo-cleanup utility for iOS that helps you find and remove duplicate, blurry, and screenshot photos and large videos to free up storage. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data we collect

None. ClearSpace does not collect personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not have servers, and your photos and videos are never uploaded anywhere.
  • We do not require an account, sign-in, or any personal information.
  • We do not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

Photo library access

To do its job, ClearSpace asks your permission to access your photo library. This access is used solely to analyze your photos and videos on your device — to detect duplicates, blurry images, screenshots, and large videos, and to delete the items you choose. The analysis happens locally using Apple's on-device frameworks. No photo, video, thumbnail, or analysis result is ever sent off your device.

When you delete items, the deletion is performed through iOS, which shows its own confirmation prompt. You are always in control of what is removed.

Purchases

ClearSpace offers a one-time, non-subscription in-app purchase ("ClearSpace Pro") to unlock unlimited bulk deletion. Purchases and restores are handled entirely by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We never receive or store your payment details. Apple's handling of transactions is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

ClearSpace does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data retention & security

Because we never collect or transmit your data, there is nothing for us to retain or expose. All app data and settings remain on your device under iOS's standard protections.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.
