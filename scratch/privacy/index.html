Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Scratch is built to be private by design. Your rounds, practice, and stats stay on your device. We do not operate servers that store your data, and there are no accounts.

What we collect

Nothing. Scratch does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There is no analytics SDK, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Your data & storage

All data you enter — rounds, scores, shots, and practice — is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. It never leaves your device unless you back it up yourself through your own iCloud device backup.

Purchases

The one-time Scratch Pro unlock is processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles payment; we never see your payment details.

Children

Scratch is rated 4+ and does not collect any data from anyone, including children.
