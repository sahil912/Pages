Scoring a match

Tap Quick Match, name the two sides, and choose a format (best of 1/3/5 and the points to win). Then tap a team's half of the scoreboard to add a point. Sets and the match are won automatically (with win-by-two). Use Undo to fix a mistake, or the back chevron to pause — your score is saved so you can resume later.

Choosing the winning score

On the match setup screen, pick the points to win from the chips (11, 15, 21, 25…). You can add or remove values in Settings → Points to win.

Running a tournament

Tap Tournaments → +, add your teams (one per line), choose how many groups, and assign teams to groups. Pick Matches per opponent to play each pairing once or twice (double round-robin). Standings update live as you score. When you're ready, open the Bracket tab to seed a knockout — winners advance automatically.

Entering a final score

Don't want to score point-by-point? Long-press a fixture (or a bracket matchup) and choose Enter final score to type the set scores directly.

Editing a completed match

Tap a finished match to see the result, then tap Edit Score and confirm to change it. Standings and the bracket update automatically.

VolleyScore Pro & purchases

Unlimited quick matches and two tournaments are free. Unlock unlimited tournaments with the one-time VolleyScore Pro purchase — no subscription. If you reinstall or get a new device, open Settings → Unlock VolleyScore Pro → Restore Purchases (or tap Restore on the unlock screen) to restore it for free.
